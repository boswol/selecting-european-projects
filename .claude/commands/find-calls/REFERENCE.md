### VLAIO Instruments — Detailed Breakdown

VLAIO runs an **annual call cycle**: most calls open in autumn (September–November) and close in November–February. All 2025-2026 round deadlines have passed by April 2026; the next wave is expected September–November 2026. Monitor https://www.vlaio.be/nl/nieuws from August 2026 for announcements.

| Instrument | URL | Relevance to profile | Typical budget | Typical deadline | Lead applicant |
|---|---|---|---|---|---|
| **LA-trajecten** (landbouw/agriculture trajectories) | https://www.vlaio.be/nl/vlaio-netwerk/landbouw-la-trajecten | **Best fit** — demand-driven applied research for agriculture & horticulture; biological control, food safety, precision farming all eligible; annual priority theme (e.g. sustainable soil management); results must be applicable in short term | €200k–€1M | Nov (annual) | Research institute (hogeschool, university, research centre) |
| **TETRA** (knowledge transfer + AI track) | https://www.vlaio.be/en/vlaio-network/tetra | Practice-oriented research translating knowledge to SMEs; **AI track (TETRA VAIOP)** active since 2025-2026, specifically funds transfer of Flemish AI Research Programme results to companies; food/agri SMEs are a strong use case | €150k–€500k | Nov (annual) | Research institute (hogeschool or university of applied sciences) |
| **COOCK+** (collective R&D + AI track) | https://www.vlaio.be/nl/subsidies-financiering/subsidiedatabank/maatregelen/coock | Collective R&D and knowledge dissemination for a sector; **AI track (COOCK+ VAIOP)** active; prior projects include drone/precision agriculture (OpsDrone: VITO + ILVO + FlandersMake); industry user committee of Flemish companies required | €300k–€1M | Feb (annual) | Research institute |
| **Baekeland mandates** | https://www.vlaio.be/en/subsidies/baekeland-mandates | PhD research in close collaboration with a Flemish company; any topic incl. AI in food/agri; ~2 calls/year (spring + autumn) | ~€200k per mandate | Mar & Sep (biannual) | Company + research institute jointly |
| **Innovation mandates** | https://www.vlaio.be/en/subsidies/innovation-mandates | Post-doc knowledge transfer or spin-off creation; ~2 calls/year | ~€150k per mandate | Mar & Sep (biannual) | Company + post-doc researcher |
| **Onderzoeksproject** (R&D subsidy) | https://www.vlaio.be/nl/subsidies-financiering/onderzoeksproject | **Rolling / no deadline** — company-led R&D projects; 25–60% subsidy on accepted costs (70% for international collaboration); AI explicitly supported; research institutes participate as paid knowledge partners but cannot be lead; budget €100k–€3M | €100k–€3M subsidy | Rolling — anytime | **Company must lead**; research institute as subcontractor |

## EU Structural & Agricultural Funds

Funded through the Common Agricultural Policy (CAP) and related instruments; access is typically via national paying agencies or managing authorities, not direct EU applications.

| Programme | URL | Key details |
|---|---|---|
| EAFRD — European Agricultural Fund for Rural Development | https://commission.europa.eu/funding-tenders/find-funding/eu-funding-programmes/european-agricultural-fund-rural-development-eafrd_en | €95.5B budget for 2021–27 (incl. €8.1B NextGenerationEU); finances national rural development programmes; research institutes participate through Operational Groups or as knowledge-transfer partners; access via national paying agencies |
| EIP-AGRI — European Innovation Partnership for Agriculture | https://ec.europa.eu/eip/agriculture/en/find-connect/funding-opportunities.html | Embedded in EAFRD; stimulates co-creation between researchers and farmers; funds Operational Groups (OGs) for applied, on-farm innovation projects; results fed back into Horizon Europe policy; calls run at national/regional level through rural development programmes |
| BiodivERsA | https://www.biodiversa.eu/ | ERA-Net co-funded partnership for biodiversity and ecosystem research; relevant for biological control and agro-ecosystem topics; runs joint transnational calls with national funders (NWO, ANR, DFG, etc.); projects typically 3 years, €500k–€2M per consortium |
