## Description

The second FutureFoodS co-funded call focuses on accelerating the transition to sustainable food systems through three themes: (1) domestic food practices enhancing sustainable and healthy diets, (2) towards diversified, sustainable, and circular food processing systems, and (3) trust and transparency in sustainable food systems. Projects can be Exploratory Research or Accelerating Innovation type; min 3 partners from ≥3 participating countries.
