# VLAIO Onderzoeksproject — Company R&D Subsidy (Rolling)

- **Programme:** VLAIO — Subsidie Onderzoeksproject (Flanders innovation & entrepreneurship)
- **Call ID:** Rolling / permanent — no fixed call deadline
- **URL:** https://www.vlaio.be/nl/subsidies-financiering/onderzoeksproject
- **Deadline:** Rolling — applications can be submitted at any time
- **Budget per project:** €100k–€3M subsidy per project (25–60% of accepted costs; 70% for inter-regional or international collaborations); project duration max 36 months
- **Action type:** Industrial R&D project (company-led)
- **Fit score:** 2/5 — budget and AI/food topics are eligible; however the lead applicant must be a company (Flemish SME or large enterprise), not a research institute; research institutes participate as knowledge partners/subcontractors

## Description

The VLAIO Onderzoeksproject subsidises Flemish companies carrying out industrial research or experimental development projects, either individually or in collaboration with research partners. There is no sectoral or topical restriction — AI, food safety, agriculture, and biological control projects are all eligible. A research institute can participate as a paid knowledge partner but cannot be the submitting/lead entity.

## Relevance to Profile

- **Topics:** food safety, food security, agriculture, AI — all fully eligible; no exclusions
- **Keywords:** AI explicitly listed as a supported domain (VLAIO has a dedicated AI subsidy track); generative AI and agentic AI tools for agri-food applications would qualify
- **Institution type:** research institutes are eligible as **subcontractors / knowledge partners**, not as lead applicants; requires finding a Flemish company co-applicant
- **Consortium size:** single company or multi-partner (no minimum); works well with a company anchor + research institute partner structure
- **Deadline constraint:** rolling — can apply at any point in 2026 or 2027; no 3-month constraint applies
- **Action to take:** identify a Flemish agri-food or AgTech company willing to co-develop an AI-in-agriculture or food-safety R&D project; they would lead the application with the research institute as knowledge partner
