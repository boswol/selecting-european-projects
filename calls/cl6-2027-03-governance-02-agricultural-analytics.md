## Description

This topic funds research to improve data collection, analytical capacity, and decision tools for farmers, agri-food supply chain actors, and policy makers, enabling better-informed decisions that balance competitiveness, sustainability, and resilience. Projects are expected to improve understanding of competitiveness determinants and their interactions with sustainability dimensions (economic, social, environmental) for the EU agricultural sector.

Projects must apply the multi-actor approach and use lump-sum funding. The Joint Research Centre (JRC) may participate as an associated partner.
