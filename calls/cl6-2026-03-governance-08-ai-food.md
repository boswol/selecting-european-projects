# Boosting Data Availability and AI Solutions in Food for Consumers and Food Service Professionals

- **Programme:** Horizon Europe – Cluster 6 (Food, Bioeconomy, Natural Resources, Agriculture and Environment)
- **Call ID:** HORIZON-CL6-2026-03-GOVERNANCE-08

- **Deadline:** 23 September 2026
- **Budget per project:** ~€7.5M (total €15M for 2 projects)
- **Action type:** Innovation Action (IA)
- **Fit score:** 5/5 — Directly targets AI solutions in food, matching both the food-safety topic cluster and the AI/LLM keywords in the research profile.

## Description

This call invites innovation actions to improve data availability and develop AI-powered tools that help consumers and food service professionals make informed, healthy, and sustainable food choices. It targets the full food information chain, from production labelling to point-of-sale digital interfaces and personalised guidance systems.

## Relevance to Profile

- **Topics matched:** food safety, food (direct), AI/artificial intelligence (direct), generative AI (applicable to consumer-facing recommendation systems)
- **Institution fit:** Research institutes eligible; Innovation Action requires a demonstration component, which suits applied research institutes with industry links
- **Budget fit:** ~€7.5M falls within the €200k–€5M preferred range only for a sub-task; consider targeting a work-package lead role to control scope
- **Consortium:** Expect 6–10 partners including food industry, consumer organisations, and tech partners — large consortium profile
