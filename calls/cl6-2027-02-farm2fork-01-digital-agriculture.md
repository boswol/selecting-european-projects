## Description

This topic funds research into digital technologies and decision support systems (DSS) to help farmers cope with water and nutrient scarcity in agricultural systems across EU pedoclimatic regions. Projects must develop and test monitoring devices and farm management systems that are affordable and interoperable with existing farm management information systems (FMIS), and deliver digital advisory tools to improve farmers' resilience and competitiveness while reducing pollution.

Proposals must apply the multi-actor approach, reach TRL 4–5 by project end, and study complex soil-water-nutrient-crop interactions across diverse conditions.
