## Description

This topic funds the development, testing, and piloting of AI-based solutions that provide comprehensive, tailored, and impartial advice to farmers, foresters, and their advisors. Projects must build on multimodal AI systems that interpret diverse data inputs (text, images, audio, video) drawn from public and private databases, in-situ measurements, and existing EU data spaces, to improve the competitiveness and sustainability of EU agriculture and forestry sectors.

Projects must implement the multi-actor approach, with a consortium including farmers, advisors, technology developers, and research organisations. Outputs must reach TRL 8 by project end. Synergy with the Common European Agriculture Data Space and AgData partnership is expected.
