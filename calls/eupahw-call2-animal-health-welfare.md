## Description

The second EUPAHW co-funded call addresses 12 Strategic Research and Innovation Agenda (SRIA) actions across two priority areas: animal welfare throughout the production cycle, and prevention and control of infectious diseases including responsible antimicrobial use. The call pools funding from 24 countries with EU co-funding under Horizon Europe.
