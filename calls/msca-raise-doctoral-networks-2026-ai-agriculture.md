# MSCA Doctoral Networks 2026 — RAISE Pilot: AI in Science (Agriculture & Environment)

- **Programme:** Horizon Europe — Marie Skłodowska-Curie Actions (MSCA) Doctoral Networks, RAISE pilot
- **Call ID:** HORIZON-MSCA-2026-DN-01 / HORIZON-RAISE-2026-01-03
- **URL:** https://marie-sklodowska-curie-actions.ec.europa.eu/calls/msca-doctoral-networks-2026
- **Deadline:** 24 November 2026, 17:00 CET (call opens 28 May 2026)
- **Budget per project:** ~€3–5M per doctoral network (€30M RAISE component; €593M total MSCA DN programme); individual institute share depends on PhD slots
- **Action type:** MSCA Doctoral Network (training + research)
- **Fit score:** 3/5 — AI-in-agriculture focus matches keywords and topics well; format is doctoral training rather than direct applied research, but generates research outputs and builds strategic capacity

## Description

MSCA Doctoral Networks 2026 fund multi-partner consortia that train PhD researchers across Europe. The RAISE pilot within this call specifically targets doctoral networks applying AI to scientific research domains, including agriculture and environmental science (building on HORIZON-RAISE-2026-01-02). Proposals flagged as RAISE-aligned during evaluation join the broader RAISE community of AI-in-science networks. The total MSCA DN budget is €593M; the RAISE-specific component is €30M.

## Relevance to Profile

- **Topics:** AI in agriculture and environmental science is the explicit RAISE focus; food safety and food security can be addressed as application domains within a network
- **Keywords:** AI, artificial intelligence — central to the RAISE pilot; generative AI and agentic AI are natural sub-topics for next-generation network proposals
- **Institution type:** research institutes are core consortium members as PhD-hosting organisations and supervisors; at least 3 partners from ≥3 countries required
- **Consortium size:** typically 5–10 institutions — fits large consortium preference (6+)
- **Deadline constraint:** November 24, 2026 is 7 months away — excellent runway; call opens May 28, leaving 6 months to prepare
- **Note:** this is a training programme, not a direct research grant; the case for entry is building a strategic pipeline of AI-in-food/agriculture researchers and producing publishable research as part of the doctoral projects
