## Description

This topic funds the development of AI algorithms capable of characterizing the "foodome" — the complete set of chemical compounds present in food samples, including thousands of poorly-characterized contaminants and endogenous biomolecules. Projects must develop validated AI tools to accelerate high-throughput foodomics data analysis (e.g., mass spectrometry), build AI-ready databases of food compounds, and improve synergies between academia, research infrastructures, and industry.

Projects must start from TRL 5 and achieve TRL 7. No multi-actor approach is required, but collaboration with research infrastructures (mass spec facilities, food databases) is expected.
