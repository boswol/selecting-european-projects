## Description

This call supports networks that use peer-to-peer, farmer-to-farmer learning and on-farm demonstration sites to accelerate the uptake of sustainable agricultural practices. Projects should include rigorous cost-benefit analysis of the demonstrated innovations and feed findings into the EU Agricultural Knowledge and Innovation Systems (AKIS).
